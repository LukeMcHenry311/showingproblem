Ravenclaw

Element: Air

Traits

Ravenclaw House prizes learning, wisdom, wit, and intellect in its members. Ravenclaws tend to be academically motivated and talented students. They also pride themselves on being original in their ideas, and methods. It is not unusual to find Ravenclaw practising different types of magic otherwise ignored.

Reputation

Ravenclaws are so competitive when it comes to academic success that they are known to backstab each other, and likely other students, in order to get top marks. Ravenclaw is so proud of their success that they claim near any intelligent wizard as a hypothetical Ravenclaw, such as Merlyn.

Founder

Rowena Ravenclaw was a Scottish witch and one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She was notable for both her intelligence and creativity. Rowena was seen as intimidating due to her considerable level of skill and proficiency in charms. Presumably it was Rowena who placed the greatest enchantments on the Sorting Hat. (or so house Ravenclaw claims.)
