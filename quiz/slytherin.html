Slytherin

Element: Water

Traits

House Slytherin tend to be ambitious, shrewd, cunning, great with python(s) 😉, strong leaders, and achievement oriented. They also have highly developed senses of self-preservation. Slytherins tend to hesitate before acting, so as to weigh all possible outcomes before deciding exactly what should be done.

Reputation

Slytherin has a well-known reputation for creating dark witches and wizards, in fact many would even say that every dark witch or wizard was actually in Slytherin. Ironically this reputation has led to a prejudice against the entire house, however some say that those who criticize students in house Slytherin purely for the house they hail from are guilty of what they claim house Slytherin is guilty of, discrimination.

Founder

Salazar Slytherin was one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, noted for his cunning and determination. He was regarded as one of the greatest wizards of the age,respectively as a Parselmouth, and as a skilled Legilimens. Salazar is most well known as the one responsible for the construction of the Chamber of Secrets beneath Hogwarts dungeons. It was specifically created for the purpose of purging the school of all Muggle-born students. The chamber contained a basilisk which could only be controlled by his own "true heir".
