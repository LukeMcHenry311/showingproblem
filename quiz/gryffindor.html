Gryffindor

Element: Fire

Traits

The Gryffindor house emphasises the traits of courage as well as daring, nerve, and chivalry. Gryffindor house members are seen as brave, although sometimes this is seen as a weakness. Some Gryffindors have also been noted to be short-tempered.

Reputation

Gryffindors and Slytherins have shared a fierce house rivalry since their founders. Godric Gryffindor and Salazar Slytherin, had a severe falling out over Slytherin's emphasis on blood purity. There might also be an element of feeling wronged, as Harry and his fellow Gryffindors tend to win in a lot of circumstances which, when viewed from a neutral point of view, may be considered unfair.

Founder

Godric Gryffindor was an English wizard and one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Godric is most well known for his legendary swordsmanship, he was so skillful that he preferred to duel muggles with his sword instead of his magic, despite magic being a far more decisive and powerful weapon.
